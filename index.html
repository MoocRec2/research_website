What is MoocRec v2?

MoocRec v2 is a MOOC (Massive Online Open Courses) search engine that suggests and lets you search thousands of courses across major platforms with your unique preference in video styles in mind.

_Personalized

MoocRec is able to identify video styles that you engage the most with.

_Review Based

We search through thousands of forum posts everyday to identify how consumers feel about particular MOOCs.

_Better Recommendations

All this is done to make sure you get the highest quality MOOCs that suits you!
